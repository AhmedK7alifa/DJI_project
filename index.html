The DJI Journey

2006: Founding

DJI was founded by Frank Wang in his university dorm room in Shenzhen, China with a small team focused on flight control technology.

2013: Phantom Vision

Launched the Phantom series with built-in cameras, making aerial photography accessible to consumers worldwide.

2015: Global Dominance

DJI captured 70% of the consumer drone market with the Phantom 3 series, establishing itself as the industry leader.

2016: Mavic Pro

Introduced the foldable Mavic Pro, revolutionizing portable drones without compromising performance.

2020: Pandemic Response

DJI drones were used globally for disinfecting public spaces and delivering medical supplies during COVID-19.

2023: Agricultural Focus

Expanded into agricultural drones, helping farmers increase yields while reducing chemical usage by 30%.
